Getting Here

A five-minute walk from the Eastsound village green and a short drive from the Orcas ferry landing. Street parking on Prune Alley and along North Beach Road.

Good to Know

House rules

21+ After 9

Open to all ages during dinner. The bar becomes 21+ each night after 9 PM — bring ID.

Dog-Friendly Patio

Leashed dogs welcome on the patio. Water bowls at the host stand. Inside is people only.

Walk-Ins Only

We don't take reservations — just show up. The wait moves fast and there's always a stool.

Cash & Card

All major cards, Apple & Google Pay, and yes — we still take cash at the bar.

Private Events

Back room seats 24 with its own bar. Email or call for availability.

Gift Cards

Available in any amount at the bar. The easy gift for the islander who already has everything.
