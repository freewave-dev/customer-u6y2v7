Live on Prune Alley

Events & Nights

From a tight trivia table to a packed karaoke floor — this is where the island comes to spend its weeknights.

The Weekly

What's on, when

Free to attend, free to play. Stick around late, the bar stays loose until 11.

Tuesdays

Trivia Night

8:00 PM — 10:00 PM

Five rounds, six categories, no phones at the table. Teams of up to six. Top three teams win bar tabs; everyone wins by ordering wings.

Thursdays

Open Mic

7:30 PM — Late

Acoustic, electric, spoken word, the occasional standup — all welcome and all encouraged. Sign-up at the bar from 6 PM. Bring your own pedals, we provide the amp and the ears.

Saturdays

Karaoke

9:00 PM — 12:00 AM

The biggest songbook on Orcas. Hosted live by KJ Dani. Two-drink minimum, zero judgment, and a long queue once the ferry crowd arrives.

Fridays & Sundays

Live Sets

8:00 PM — 10:30 PM

Rotating local bookings — folk, bluegrass, country-rock and the odd jazz duo. No cover. Tip the band and they'll come back.

“On a Thursday in January, the open mic at the Lower is the warmest room on the island.” — Islands' Weekly
House Rules

A few things to know

Age

21+ After 9 PM

Family-friendly during dinner service. Bar transitions to 21+ each night after 9.

Patio

Dog-Friendly

Leashed dogs welcome on the patio. Water bowls and biscuits on the house.

Seating

First Come, First Served

We don't take reservations on event nights. Show up early to lock in a table.

Private Events

Book the back room.

Birthdays, wakes, wrap parties, fantasy football drafts — the back room seats 24 with a private bar, a sound system, and the same menu as out front. Email or call to ask about availability.
