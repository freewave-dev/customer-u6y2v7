The Lower Tavern is where Orcas Islanders and visitors come together for great food, cold drinks, and the games we all love. Pull up a stool, settle in, and stay a while.

Three decades on the island and still the easiest place to find a friendly face, a cold pint, and whatever game happens to be on.

The Lower is a 21+ tavern — no babies, please. Bring your ID and make yourself at home.
