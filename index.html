Our Story

Three decades of last calls, first dates, and second helpings.

The Lower Tavern has been a fixture on Prune Alley since 1994 — a working pub built on cold beer, hot griddles, and the kind of crowd that knows everyone's name by Wednesday.

We're proudly local, proudly loud some nights and proudly quiet on others. Whether you're docking in from the ferry or you've been coming since the original lease, you'll find the same honest plates, the same long bar, and the same low light that's been here all along.
